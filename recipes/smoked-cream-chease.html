Smoked Cream Cheese

Looking for a delicious smoked appetizer? Here is the recipe to my crowd pleasing Smoked Cream Cheese!

Ingredients

  • Block of Cream Cheese
  • Favorite AP and BBQ seasoning
  • Crackers, pretzels, or whatever you want to dip

Instructions

  1. Prepare smoker to 250°F - 275°F
  2. Add favorite wood, chips, or pellets. I use a blend of maple and cherry woods
  3. Take block of cream cheese out of the fridge. Score diagonally. Rotate one quarter and repeat
  4. Apply your favorite BBQ seasoning liberally. Cover all surfaces
  5. Place Cream Cheese in smoker for 2 - 2½ hours
  6. Remove Cream Cheese and serve with your favorite dippers
  7. You can even pre-smoke, place in fridge, and reheat in oven when you are ready
